Lemon Herb Chicken

Ingredients and recipe steps.

Ingredients:

  • 300 g chicken thighs
  • 30 ml lemon juice
  • 10 g garlic
  • 5 g rosemary
  • 250 g potatoes

Recipe Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with lemon, garlic, and rosemary.
  2. Arrange chicken and potatoes in a baking tray.
  3. Bake until chicken is golden and potatoes are tender.
  4. Serve hot with extra lemon juice.
