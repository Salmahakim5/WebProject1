Tuna Protein Bowl

Ingredients and recipe steps.

Ingredients:

  • 160 g tuna
  • 250 g cooked brown rice
  • 80 g corn
  • 90 g avocado
  • 20 ml lemon juice

Recipe Steps

  1. Cook brown rice and place in a serving bowl.
  2. Add tuna, corn, and sliced avocado on top.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  4. Mix lightly and serve immediately.
