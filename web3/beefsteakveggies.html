Beef Steak with Veggies

Ingredients and recipe steps.

Ingredients:

  • 220 g beef steak
  • 15 ml olive oil
  • 3 g black pepper
  • 120 g carrots
  • 100 g green beans

Recipe Steps

  1. Season steak with salt and black pepper.
  2. Sear in a hot pan to preferred doneness.
  3. Saute carrots and green beans in olive oil.
  4. Serve steak with the vegetables.
